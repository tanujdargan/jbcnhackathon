Frequently Asked Questions

1
What is a Hackathon?

A hackathon is a sprint-like design event wherein computer programmers and others involved in software development, including graphic designers, interface designers, product managers, project managers and others collaborate intensively under a on software projects.

2
Why should I participate?

If you are looking to build your portfolio, gain experience or gain an insight into the IT industry this event is for you. The event is specially tailored for students

3
Who is eligible to attend?

Participants between grades 8 to 12 can take part in the event.

4
Do we need to have coding knowledge?

We recommend having some background in computing and programming languages. These would help you create a more convincing end-product to present to the judges.

5
Is there a participation fee?

Our hackathon is 100% free for all participants, there is no registration fees.

6
What if I don't have a team/ Solo Participation

In this situation you will be working alone if you are comfortable with that, you are free to do However if you wish to be part of team, you can contact us and we will assign you to a team, from our Discord server.
