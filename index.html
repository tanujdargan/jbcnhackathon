A hackathon is an event based solely on programming and the practicality of the project itself. All participants should note that the presentation aspect of your project is as important as the coding aspect of your project. No drag-drop based coding is permitted throughout the course of this event. (E.g. Scratch, Touch Develop, Tinkercad, Website Building tools, MIT App Inventor). Your projects must be submitted on GitHub under a public repository. A GitHub tutorial will be given as soon as the beginning of the event and participants are requested to attend if they wish to do so. Over the course of the three days, participants will have 48 hours to come up with a project under one of the themes.
